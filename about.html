Our Story

Founded in 2016 by Mr. Govinda Raju, Raj Marketing started as a small workshop in Hootghalli, Mysore. With a vision to provide affordable and high-quality mosquito net solutions to every home, we have grown into one of Mysore's most trusted mosquito net manufacturers and installers.

What began as a one-man operation has now expanded into a team of 25+ skilled professionals, serving thousands of satisfied customers across Mysore and surrounding areas.

"Our mission is simple - to protect every family from mosquitoes while ensuring comfort and style. We believe that quality shouldn't be a luxury, it should be accessible to all."

- Govinda Raju, Founder
