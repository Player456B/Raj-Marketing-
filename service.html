Frequently Asked Questions

How long does installation take?
Standard installation takes 2-4 hours depending on the number of windows/doors. Velcro systems are faster (1-2 hours), while frame systems may take 3-4 hours.
What is your warranty period?
We offer 2 years warranty on velcro systems, 5 years on frame systems, and 3 years on pleated nets. All installations come with 1 year service warranty.
Do you provide emergency service?
Yes! We offer 24/7 emergency repair services. Call our emergency helpline at 9483037385 for immediate assistance.
How to claim warranty?
You can claim warranty by visiting our service page, calling our support number, or visiting our office with the original bill and warranty card.
What areas do you serve?
We serve all areas of Mysore including Vijayanagar, Kuvempunagar, Gokulam, Jayalakshmipuram, Hebbal, and surrounding areas.
